Spotlight is a responsive theme that puts the 'Spotlight' on your posts while massively simplifying customizing choices. There are 4 customizable colours ensuring blog cohesiveness. There are 6 navigation links in total, 2 on the landing page and the remaining 4 in the pop up tab.

Spotlight utilizes infinite scroll and masonry to display images and other media in a gallery type manner. This minimizes page load time and maintains a professional atmosphere.
