Open UToronto is a responsive website, hosted on WordPress. WordPress's use of PHP enables the content within the site to be constantly updated with the addition of new projects and resources. The overall aesthetic is based on the University of Toronto's official Style Guide.

Open UToronto utilizes PHP and Javascript to provide a dynamic and intuitive experience to browsing its content. The use of appropriate media queries as well as following AODA requirements ensure that the website's experience will be standardized, regardless of the visitor's visiting device of choice.
