Toronto Sock Exchange is a non-profit organization that seeks to provide socks to the homeless population of Toronto. They achieve this through selling 'fancy' socks among corporations and using the profits to buy warm socks to donate to homeless shelters.

They required a static website with which to provide basic information on their organization; they collaborated with a designer in Developers' Foundation to create a wireframe of the idea.

My role as Software Developer was to use a combination of LESS, HTML5 and some basic Javascript to create the static webpage of their minimalistic design, while working with four teammates to achieve the final product.
